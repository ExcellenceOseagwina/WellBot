About the assistant

University guidance in one student portal

The assistant helps Wellspring University students quickly find guidance on admissions, academic processes, campus services, technical support, and general student questions.

Built around student needs

Students can ask questions in everyday language, including imperfect spelling, and receive responses from a Wellspring University knowledge base. When information depends on the current academic session, the assistant points students to the correct official office, portal, or timetable.

Covered offices

Admissions, registry, bursary, ICT support, student affairs, library, departments, and academic advisers.
